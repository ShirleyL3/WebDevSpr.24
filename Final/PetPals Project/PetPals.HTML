PetPals

Figma User Research User Flow Prototyping Iteration

Overview:

Create a pet care service for owners to easily track their pets's health, book sitters/daycare, and connect with vets and other pet owners instantly, ensuring convenient and reliable care for their pets

MindMap:

All the possible aspects and features that would be added to PetPals were brainstromed into a clear map that categorized it into smaller categories. Some possible features included a Health Tracker, a Pet Supply Marketplace, a Personalized Pet Profile, and other general Pet Services.

User Task Flow:

All the possible aspects and features that would be added to PetPals were brainstromed into a clear map that categorized it into smaller categories. Some possible features included a Health Tracker, a Pet Supply Marketplace, a Personalized Pet Profile, and other general Pet Services.

Client Research:

To gather general information on clients a survey was used including questions such as:

  • How many pets do you own?
  • What kind of pet(s) o you have?
  • How do you keep track of your pet's care?
  • What specific challenges do you face in managing your pet's care?
  • What features would be most useful in a pet care app?

Results:

From the client interviews, it was learned that majority of pet owners own dogs with almsot half of the clients soley owning a singular pet. More than 35+ clients agreed that a pet health tracker to track vaccinations, treatments, and weight.
